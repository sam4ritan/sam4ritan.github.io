What do I do?

Well, coding is what I do, from time to time, to say the least. More often than not it's scripts in python, ruby or for one of the many shells. Also, I do maintain some java/android-based projects and some simple webdesign (which I do not really like), but I mostly create scripts for stress-testing, automation and similar tasks.

The latter are not here on GitHub but on GitLab, because I do not want them to fall into the hands of some clueless scriptkiddie, only to blow up in their face and fry their hardware.

Additionally, I tend to know my way around with most operating sytems (including some rather esoteric stuff like Windows 8 or Emacs) but I usually prefer linux-based OS.
